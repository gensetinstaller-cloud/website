The Problem

Why unfamiliarity with generator models is a critical risk.

Site inspection is the most crucial stage of the project. When teams lack deep familiarity with specific generator models, serious complications arise during installation:

  • Incorrect installation planning leading to rework costs.
  • Inadequate space allocation for positioning and maintenance.
  • Incompatible fuel and electrical connections.
  • Breakdowns in communication with customers.
  • Severe safety risks to personnel and infrastructure.
